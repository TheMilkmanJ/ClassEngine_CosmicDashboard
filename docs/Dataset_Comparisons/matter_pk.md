# The matter power spectrum

*The model's linear and nonlinear P(k) at z = 0 track ΛCDM across the clustering range, with
σ8 = 0.817 and S8 = 0.815. Code: `scripts/matter_pk.py`; figure `chains/matter_pk.png`.*

## The clustering

The model's matter power spectrum runs with ΛCDM's across the linear and nonlinear regimes, from the
turnover near k ~ 0.01 to the small scales where halofit sets the amplitude, the two agreeing to a few
percent across the decade the galaxy surveys resolve.

## S8

The model has σ8 = 0.817, Ω_m = 0.299, and S8 = σ8 √(Ω_m/0.3) = 0.815. That is below ΛCDM's 0.832 —
the lower matter density carries it down — and it sits near the Planck value 0.834 ± 0.016, above the
weak-lensing band (DES-Y3 0.776 ± 0.017, KiDS-1000 0.759 ± 0.021). The model moves S8 toward the
lensing values without reaching them; a mild offset remains, smaller than ΛCDM's.
