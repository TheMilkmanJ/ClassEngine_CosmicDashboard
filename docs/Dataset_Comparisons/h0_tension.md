# The H0 landscape

*The model's varying-mₑ raises the CMB-inferred H0 from Planck's 67.4 to 69.6, closing 40% of the gap
to the SH0ES distance-ladder value. Code: `scripts/h0_tension.py`; figure `chains/h0_tension.png`.*

## The determinations

Early (sound-horizon) H0 clusters near 67–68: Planck 2018 CMB 67.36 ± 0.54, ACT+WMAP 67.6 ± 1.1,
BBN+BAO without the CMB 67.4 ± 1.1. Late (distance-ladder) H0 clusters near 73: SH0ES Cepheids
73.04 ± 1.04, TDCOSMO lensing time delays 73.3 ± 1.8, with TRGB intermediate at 69.8 ± 1.7. The two
camps sit 5.7 km s⁻¹ Mpc⁻¹ apart, a 4.8σ separation.

## Where the model sits

Varying-mₑ = 1 + 27α/5π = 1.0125 shifts recombination, and the CMB-inferred H0 with it: the model's
derived stack gives H0 = 69.6, up 2.25 km s⁻¹ Mpc⁻¹ from the Planck value. That is 40% of the way
across the early–late gap, and it lowers the tension against SH0ES from 4.8σ to 2.9σ. The model moves
the early value toward the late one without reaching it; a 2.9σ separation from the ladder remains.
