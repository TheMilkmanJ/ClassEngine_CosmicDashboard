## 0. The paradox, and why it needs a singularity to bite

Hawking's paradox: radiation carries no information; the singularity destroys the infallen
state; when the hole evaporates, a pure state has evolved to a mixed one — unitarity dies.
Every horn of the dilemma leans on one premise: **that the interior terminates histories**
(the singularity as an information shredder).
