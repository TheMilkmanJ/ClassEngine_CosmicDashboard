## Worked example — why cosmic birefringence can't clear (L1 in action)

*The clearest demonstration of L1 — the "clear it through gravity or EM?"
question (2026-07-08). Cross-refs the graveyard entry "model-SOURCED cosmic
birefringence" (8 angles) in `PRTOE_INTERACTION_ATLAS.md`.*

Cosmic birefringence is a **parity-odd effect on photon polarization** — rotating
it treats left-circular differently from right. Trace it through the coupling law:

- **Channel 1 — Gravity (L1, the medium's ONLY universal coupling).** Gravity is
  identity-blind — it couples to **energy/mass**, nothing else. It carries a
  *mass* effect fine (that's R1, the m_e shift). But it has **no handedness
  coupling** (the metric is parity-even). A handedness effect has nothing to act on
  → acts parity-even only (redshift/lensing), never a rotation.
- **Channel 2 — EM (the sector that DOES carry handedness).** Photon polarization *lives*
  in the EM sector, so EM is the right channel. But coupling through EM requires
  **EM charge**, and the medium is **EM-neutral** (dark, by
  constitution). No charge, no coupling. *This is the census.*

**Net:** birefringence is an effect in a channel the medium's only universal coupling (gravity)
doesn't carry, in the sector that does carry it (EM) but where the medium has no
charge. **Forbidden by coupling structure, not by fiat.** The medium *watches*
the polarization form through gravity, but gravity is handedness-blind
— it has no way to act on it. (Timing intuition preserved: recombination
IS when the EM handedness-window opens — but it opens for EM-charged matter, the
charged matter doing Thomson scattering; the medium isn't one.)

### The transfer refinement — "transfer it EM → gravity"

There IS a link between the sectors — EM has stress-energy and stress-energy
gravitates (light bends space). But what crosses is only the **parity-even
part**: gravity couples to T_μν^EM = F_μα F_ν^α − ¼ g_μν F²,
**every term parity-even**; the handedness invariant **F·F̃ is topological and does
NOT appear in T_μν**, so it never sources the metric. **Transfer of handedness
= 0** — the energy crosses, the handedness stays in the EM sector. Both directions
shut for different reasons: EM→gravity, gravity can't *carry* a parity-odd
source; gravity→EM (what making birefringence needs), coupling to the EM field
needs the EM charge the neutral medium lacks. **This is why R1 WORKS by contrast:**
mass is parity-even, sits in T_μν, so the medium couples it through gravity
(→ the m_e win). Same link, same channel — mass couples through, handedness doesn't; the
only difference is the parity of the effect.

### β (the read gain) — is it the bridge?

β is the **matter→space return leg** ("space+matter do gravity, matter+matter do
EM, matter+space do β"), the read gain that CLOSES the m_e coupling
(space→mass→mass→**β**→space). Natural to ask if β is the inter-sector link for
handedness. It is not: **β is parity-EVEN.** It reads Θ — matter's *clustering /
saturation* state (a scalar, no handedness) — and writes it onto m_e (a mass). So β
carries the same parity-even character as gravity; it is the third leg of the
coupling that WORKS (R1), not a handedness bridge. To make β read the photon's

parity-even). So β doesn't rescue birefringence; it's more evidence of the same
rule — every legal leg the medium has (gravity, β) is parity-even, and handedness
lives in the one sector (EM) the medium has no charge in.

Can the effect be FAKED from parity-even parts? No — by symmetry, not by model
limitation. **A parity-odd observable in a parity-even state is exactly ZERO:** β
flips sign under parity, so in a parity-symmetric setup β = −β ⇒ β = 0. Cosmic
birefringence (the ⟨EB⟩ correlation) IS parity-odd, so β = 0 in ANY parity-even
theory. To get β ≠ 0 needs parity genuinely broken, two doors only: **explicit**
