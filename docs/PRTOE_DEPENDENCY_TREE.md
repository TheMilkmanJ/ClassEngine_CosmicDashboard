# THE DEPENDENCY TREE — what is conditional on what (2026-07-13)

*The transparency map the corpus owed: every major claim's parents, so a reader can trace "this dies
if that fails" without reading the derivation log. Claims inherit the **weakest** status among their
parents.*

*Six grades, written out. **Production** — fit-grade, chain-backed. **Derived** — falls out of prior
pieces, with its conditions named. **Estimate** — computed lightly, honesty band attached.
**Candidate** — mechanism exhibited, not closed. **Registered** — a bet placed before its deciding
data. **Adopted** — taken from the literature, not claimed.*

*(Grades appear as words here and everywhere else. Single letters are not used: the same letter
carried different meanings in different files — [R] was both a registered bet and ridden physics,
[P] both production-grade and parked — which is a good way to make a reader think the model claims
credit for standard physics.)*
