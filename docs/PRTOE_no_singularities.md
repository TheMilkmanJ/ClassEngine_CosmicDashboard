## 1. The claim in one sentence

**General relativity's pathologies — the black-hole singularity, the Big-Bang singularity, the
information paradox, traversable wormholes and closed timelike curves — are not five separate
problems; they are five statements about the metric at scales where, in this model, there is no
metric.** The metric is the medium's collective description; it exists only for gradients longer
than the healing length ξ = ħ/(m c_s). Below ξ there is only the quantum medium, and a
"singularity" — a claim about the geometry at arbitrarily small scales — has no referent. One
cutoff removes them all.

**Crossover mass (r_s = ξ): M ≈ 2.0×10¹⁰ M☉.** Every stellar and nearly every supermassive
black hole is *smaller than one healing length* — its entire interior is a single
quantum-coherent region, one quantum object wearing a classical horizon. Only the very largest
holes (M ≳ 2×10¹⁰ M☉; TON 618 is one) exceed it — a genuine, dated crossover the SMBH
mass-function top can test.

## 4. Why it is one move, not five patches

The resolution is the model's own **no-double-counting** principle: a degree of freedom
dissolves at its cutoff, and questions posed beneath the cutoff are category errors. The *same*
move tames the two other famous divergences the corpus already handles — the 10¹²⁰ vacuum-energy
catastrophe (the mode sum read without the equilibrium that cancels it;
[PRTOE_cosmological_constant.md](PRTOE_cosmological_constant.md)) and the graviton's
non-renormalizability (gravity is induced, not fundamental;
[PRTOE_quantum_gravity.md](PRTOE_quantum_gravity.md)). Singularities are the *geometric* face of
the same error: extrapolating an emergent description past the scale where it holds. That the
Penrose and Hawking theorems then fail is not evasion — their premises (a Lorentzian metric on
the full domain; the energy conditions on the relevant congruences) are exactly what the quantum
core removes.

## 5. What is genuinely owed

The structural claim — one cutoff, every GR pathology — is complete. What remains is one number
and one optional observable, both pre-existing in the component files; this synthesis adds no new
debt:
- **The black-hole entropy area law** from the medium: *located* (the entropy lives in the
  thermal Landau-broken core) and **the scaling is now derived** — counting horizon
  entanglement across the medium's own coherence cells against induced gravity's inverse
  Newton constant, the species count and the cutoff cancel in the ratio, which is the
  universality itself. **The coefficient is now also derived** — it is not a free bit-per-cell
  value but the *ratio* of the two heat-kernel coefficients the same cutoff supplies: induced
  Newton at 1/G = N/(12πε²) against horizon entanglement at S = N·A/(48πε²), whose ratio is
  **12π/48π = 1/4 exactly**, giving S = A/4G ([PRTOE_quantum_gravity.md](PRTOE_quantum_gravity.md)
  §4a). **The same payment closes the Page curve**, which was blocked on it. **What that
  derivation is conditional on, stated as its source states it:** 12π and 48π are the heat-kernel
  coefficients *of minimally coupled scalars*, and the cancellation is derived for that field
  content. It does **not** extend to the roster the model actually carries — for non-minimal
  coupling and for fields with spin the two divergences stop being one heat-kernel term, so the
  cancellation is not automatic. The recorded fermion-sign hazard, a fermion-dominated roster
  driving naive 1/G wrong-signed, is untouched by the ratio argument, because a ratio survives
  both its halves being wrong. **The roster question is the open item, and it is larger than the
  O(1) regulator check it replaced** — so both the area law's coefficient and the Page-curve
  closure that rides on it inherit it.
- **λ (the quartic self-coupling)** — sets the core floor and the bounce density ρ_bounce. The

  Planck density and ~21 below nuclear, which is all the "no singularity" claim requires

## 6. Falsification

The wing dies with its parents and its own sharp edges: (i) a confirmed curvature singularity or
information loss *below* ξ (there is none to have); (ii) a confirmed **local** white-hole
transient — a black-to-white remnant — which would break the global-arrow law
([PRTOE_white_holes.md](PRTOE_white_holes.md)); (iii) traversable-wormhole exotic matter at
engineering scale (the NEC floor forbids it, 17 orders under); (iv) the crossover-mass structure
failing to appear at the SMBH mass-function top; (v) trivially with M3 — no medium, no cutoff,
no cores. The exteriors are untouched throughout: horizons, shadows, and the ringdown spectra
GR predicts all stand (the model changes only what replaces r = 0).
