# The 3.5×10¹⁷ GeV "portal scale" — RESOLVED, dead relic, retired

**Closed. No decision needed after all** — the archaeology settled it.

## What it was

Both detection files justified their nulls with "the portal at ~3.5×10¹⁷ GeV (geometric mean)".

## Where it came from

It entered with the earliest threading sweep, sourced as **"def357's geometric mean"** — a record
number from the tag system retired in the hygiene pass. **def357 survives nowhere**, in the tree or
the history, and no definition of the number survives either. Every other "geometric mean" in the
corpus is a different object (the boost-dressed cutoff, the matched-junction impedance, the
conformal window's 48 keV, a velocity mean).

## What it actually is

It reconstructs to three digits as **√(M_Pl · 10¹⁶ GeV) = 3.494×10¹⁷ GeV** — the Planck–GUT
geometric mean. **The model has no GUT scale.** The dependency tree, the quantum-gravity file and
the UV-completion file never mention one. It was a placeholder pointing at a rung the framework
does not build — from before the portal was located at TeV energies by the two-loop shooter and
the high-f adoption.

## Retired

Both files now rest their nulls on scales the model derives — the dyad's decay constant at
100–500 TeV and the census portal at 13–20 TeV, both beyond collider reach. Conclusions unchanged,
reasoning sourced, and the contradiction with the referee calendar is gone.

## Worth keeping from this

Its two homes were the two files whose whole content is a prediction of *silence*. A scale that
large makes every null trivially true, so nothing downstream ever strained against it. **An
assumption that makes its own conclusions easier is the one least likely to be examined.**
